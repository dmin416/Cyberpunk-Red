# Flavor — Gig 5

Timed missing-persons rescue. Pair with [Premise.md](Premise.md) · [Layout.md](Layout.md).

---

## Where

**Stack Nine** — Rancho Coronado’s ragged edge. Cargo containers stacked three-high, CHOOH² haze, the last asphalt before scrub and dust. **Last Stop** (Rio’s truck) is parked in a permanent bay of wire-spool tables and mismatched stools.

**The Crosscut** — white stucco cube hotel one dusty block inland: 1F commercial (PI office, laundromat, Nomad waystation closet), cubes above.

**The wreck** — Badlands ridge ~1 mile north; landslide rubble on a beige Zonda Metrocar; stubby tree campsite a few hundred yards off.

Threat: **Low combat / High consequence.** Trauma doesn’t come out here for voucher pay.

## Conclusion beats

### Memorial
Candles on a spool table. Quiet. Reef transfers the partial. Rio slides vouchers over without meeting eyes. Stack Nine still says good morning — slightly quieter.

### Celebration
Clinic-pale Juno and Hale on stools. Reef pays full Hazardous. Rio announces free Koff Pop for the Crew "until this truck rusts shut." Someone starts a bad acoustic song; let it be bad.

### Aftercare (optional)
Hand Juno’s concert chip to any Media/Rocker contact — seed a gig, not a plot mandate.
