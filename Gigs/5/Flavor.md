# Flavor — Until Morning (Gig 5)

Timed missing-persons rescue. Pair with [Premise.md](Premise.md) · [Layout.md](Layout.md) · [Friends.md](Friends.md).

---

## Where

**Stack Nine** — Rancho Coronado’s ragged edge. Containers stacked three-high, CHOOH² haze, the last asphalt before scrub and dust. **Last Stop** sits in a permanent bay of wire-spool tables and mismatched stools. Breakfast line forms before the generators fully warm up.

**The Crosscut** — white stucco cube hotel one dusty block inland: 1F commercial (PI office, laundromat, Nomad waystation closet), cubes above. Smells like detergent and old coffee.

**The wreck** — Badlands ridge ~1 mile north; landslide rubble on a beige Zonda Metrocar; stubby tree campsite a few hundred yards off. Trauma doesn’t come out here for voucher pay.

**Threat:** Low combat / High consequence.

## Crosscut beats

**Office:** Sepia cosplay — faux leather, dirty glass, bourbon that isn’t bad. Bright Agent in the drawer looks like it belongs to a different movie.

**Unsent draft (read aloud if they find it):**

> Found the tree. Dust’s coming. If I’m late—

**Juno’s room:** Color explosion; drum kit eating half the floor; unfinished *Carrion & Crawlers* character sheet on the desk with only Rio’s name under “party.”

**Song title on Agent:** *Ridge That Eats Headlights* — let a Rocker PC feel that in their teeth.

## Wreck beats

When Perception hits life signs:

> Something ticks under the rubble. Not metal — breath. Thin. Not enough of it.

If they’re late (Amber/Night):

> The Metrocar’s a kiln. Whatever’s left inside is cooking in its own air.

If explosives are on the table, say it once out loud as GM: *miss this Check and you are writing their obituaries.*

---

## Conclusion beats

### Memorial
Candles on a spool table. Quiet. Reef transfers the partial. Rio slides vouchers over without meeting eyes. Stack Nine still says good morning — slightly quieter.

### Celebration
Clinic-pale Juno and Hale on stools. Reef pays full Hazardous. Rio announces free Koff Pop for the Crew "until this truck rusts shut." Someone starts a bad acoustic song; let it be bad. Hale raises a paper cup like a toast he doesn’t have words for.

### Aftercare (optional)
Hand Juno’s concert chip to any Media/Rocker contact — seed a gig, not a plot mandate. Woodchipper’s next Night Market has an extra plate of street corn with the Crew’s name on it.
