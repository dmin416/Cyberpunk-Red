## Premise

**Rio Mendez** runs **Last Stop**, a food truck parked permanent in **Stack Nine** — a container village on the **Rancho Coronado** fringe where the pavement gives up and the Badlands start.

Her regular (and current crush), Rockerboy **Juno Vale**, skipped Koff Pop two mornings running. She hired local PI **Marcus Hale** out of the **Crosscut** cube hotel. Hale hasn’t checked in either.

Rio can’t float Hazardous cash alone. **Reef** (and through him Nomad Fixer **Woodchipper**, who uses Hale) covers the **street payout** if the Crew brings people back — or proves they burned the clock trying. Rio still puts up **meal vouchers** and gratitude.

**There is no shootout waiting.** The opposition is **time**, the sun, dust, rubble, and two bleeding people under a car. If the Crew doesn’t free and stabilize them **by morning**, both die. Timer + split rescue/stabilize is what keeps this **Hazardous**.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Tech (Basic Tech / Elec/Sec / Drive)** — cube locks, levers/fulcrums on rubble, tow plans, jury-rig
- **Medtech (First Aid / Paramedic / Medicine)** — both victims at **0 HP**, unstabilized, stacked Critical Injuries
- **Perception / Tracking / Wilderness Survival / Library Search** — campsite → tracks → wreck
- **Tactics** — spiral search vs wandering until sundown
- **Social (Conversation / Persuasion)** — locals talk easier if the Crew’s already been good neighbors
- **Endurance** — walking the Badlands under midday sun

## CLIMAX — Search & Rescue

**Nav to campsite** (with Hale’s map notes): **DV13** Library Search, Local Expert (Badlands), or Wilderness Survival. Walk = **under 1 hour**. Walking under the sun: **DV13 Endurance** or **−2** to all Checks until a full night’s sleep.

Campsite is broken down — cold fire ring only. Dust ate the bike/car tracks.

| Method | DV / time |
|--------|-----------|
| Tracking | **DV15** |
| Perception (tracks) | **DV17** |
| Tactics → spiral search | **DV15** → find car in **~30 min** |
| Random wandering | Find car in **~1 more hour** |

### The wreck

Hale’s Metrocar is **pinned under landslide rubble** at a ridge base. **DV15 Perception** → signs of life inside. Rubble blocks casual reach.

**Freeing them** (player plans; sample DVs):

| Plan | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Hand-move rubble | Needs **2+** people with **BODY 10+** working together; no Check |
| Tow with vehicle | **DV17 Drive** if Heavy Chassis; **DV24** without. Fail = rubble still moves, **vehicle takes 3d6 damage** (GM: or 1 Serious damage step) |
| Explosives | ≥2 grenades (or equiv.) + **DV24 Demolitions**. **Fail = both NPCs die** |
| Levers / fulcrums | Right gear + **DV21 Basic Tech** |
| Call help | **Reef / Woodchipper** Nomad assist, or **Kel Sato** (local builder with a **GRAF3**). Social **DV13–17** by relationship + urgency |

### Medical (once free)

Both are at **0 HP**, **not stabilized**. Don’t move them until stabilized (CP:R healing rules).

| Victim | Critical Injuries (sample — adjust to taste) |
|--------|-----------------------------------------------|
| **Marcus Hale** | Broken Ribs · Cracked Skull · Whiplash |
| **Juno Vale** | Broken Leg · Foreign Object · Spinal Injury · Whiplash |

Crits don’t all need field surgery before transport once HP is stabilized — but Spinal / Cracked Skull should feel urgent for a Medtech.

→ Any death → **Memorial**  
→ Both live → **Celebration**

## Resolutions

### Memorial
Neighborhood vigil. Reef still cuts the **partial Hazardous** check (see payout table). Rio honors **7 vouchers each** but goes cold. Locals don’t blame out loud; eyes do.

### Celebration
Days later: thank-you at Last Stop after a cheap clinic stay. Reef pays **full clear**. Rio bumps to **10 vouchers each** + **free Koff Pop mornings** until the truck dies. Juno is a recruitable musician contact if the Crew wants one.
