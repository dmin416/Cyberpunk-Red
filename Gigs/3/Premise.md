# Gig 3

**Contractor:** None (personal / Tyger hunt)

**GM:** Void

**For:** Marshal (solo; optional second player / Breach)

**Rating:** NSFW

**Focus:** Combat

**Gig Difficulty:** Lethal (Street)

**Player Count:** 1 (scale up if 2)

**Payout:** Loot only — up to **5,000eb** salvage (Lethal Street cash band) · **300 IP** · **4 rep** (full clear: Claws down, woman alive)

**Type:** Hunt / intervention — two combat locations, one premise

## Premise

Marshal is **hunting Tyger Claws** in Kabuki — already slipping toward cyberpsychosis, looking for a fight that feels like justice.

He sees half-masks **dragging a woman through a doorway** (side entrance to a pachinko parlor / walk-up). They clock him. Some peel off to **hold the entryway**; others **break upstairs with the woman** while the street fight starts.

No fixer. No pay. Two fights, one chase: clear the door, then the room she's in — before backup turns Lethal into a funeral.

**GM spice (optional):** The "snatch" may be sitters extracting **Aya Kurosawa** (captain's daughter) after a rival play — see [Flavor.md](Flavor.md). Play it as a real kidnapping if you want a shallower night; the combat structure stays the same either way.

## Combat flow (Void)

```
[See woman dragged through doorway]
        │
        ▼
 WAVE 1 — Entryway (street / door / first stairs)
   Hold team fights Marshal
   Escort team is already going up with the woman
        │
        ├─ Wave 1 NOT cleared by Round 4? → REINFORCEMENTS (street)
        │
        ▼
 WAVE 2 — Upstairs room (woman is here)
   Break the door; fight whoever made it up with her
        │
        └─ Still too easy? → HARD BACKUP (LT + Mini-Boss ± mook)
```

### Scale

| Players | Opening pool | Hard backup (if crushing it) |
|---------|--------------|------------------------------|
| **1 (Marshal alone)** | **3 Hardened Mooks + 1–2 Hardened Lieutenants**, staggered across Wave 1 / escort | Optional: +1 Hardened LT **or** treat hard backup lightly |
| **2** | Same opening | **+1 Hardened LT + 1 Hardened Mini-Boss + maybe 1 Hardened Mook** |

### Round timer

| Clock | Trigger |
|-------|---------|
| **Round 4** of Wave 1 still not cleared | **Reinforcements** arrive at the street/door — 1–2 Hardened Mooks (or 1 Hardened LT if solo is steamrolling). They join Wave 1 or cut off retreat. |
| Wave 2 going too clean (no PC Seriously Wounded, enemies folding in ≤2 Rounds) | **Hard backup** — see scale table. Enter from stair / street / adjoining room. |

Stagger freely: don't dump the whole roster on Round 1.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Solo / FBC** — door breach, stairwell, room clear
- **Athletics / Evasion** — chase upstairs while under fire
- **Perception / Tactics** — clock who went up with the woman vs who stayed
- **Resist Torture / Drugs** — stay functional on the cusp
