## Read-aloud by station
Use one block when they enter. Cut short if combat starts mid-sentence.

### Lot
Night air and diesel. Flood lights bleach the fence line. Camera domes sit on poles and eaves. A side steel door has a badge reader. Tall dock doors face the cold bay. A second tall door sits beside them with an amber lamp. A van waits at the lot edge.

### Cold Dock
You smell diesel and cold plastic. Truck engines idle outside. Sealed racks hold dark shapes that do not move. A PA chime echoes. An amber lamp burns over a heavy door marked BIOSECURE.

### Wrap
You smell hot plastic. A carousel whines. Film tunnels run toward the dock. Clamp arms twitch on rails.

### Package
You smell expanding foam. Empty grade trays wait on the belt. Injectors hiss.

### Sear
You smell char and propane. Blue flame cones sweep across the belt. Grease films the floor.

### Cook
You smell hot plastic and cooked fat. Microwaver heads sit on booms. A mesh curtain shimmers.

### Portion
You smell warm fat. Dice-cutters chatter. Cubed product moves in wet chunks. The lane is narrow.

### Electrotenderize
The wet grate steams. Electrodes hang in a tunnel. Current crackles when the arch cycles. Your fillings ache.

### Pens / Kill floor
You smell fear-sweat, ozone, and iron. Chain pens line the wall. Livestock tags hang on cages. People are inside some of them. Two industrial horns aim at a yellow stun gate. A captive-bolt gantry frames the belt. Beyond it, a breakdown saw screams on the primary slice rail.

### Maint / Lockers
Metal lockers stand open. Work clothes hang half out. Dark smears run along the grate toward the pens. A safety poster reads WILD GAME / SCOP ONLY.

## Extra sensory (only when it happens)
**Sonic boom:** The horns fire. Air punches your ears. The pens ring.
**In a cook beam:** Heat builds under your gear. Chrome ticks.
**Sear cone:** Flame heat hits your face.
**Rails (casual look up):** Ceiling tracks carry arms above the belt. A thicker rail runs down the middle.
**Heavy Carrier (when it arrives):** A long clank. A cage drops onto the center rail.
**Fire:** Orange under the grease grate. PA keeps chiming.
**Bulkhead incinerator:** Heat blooms from the pens wing. Cheerful PA thanks them for pathogen compliance. Screams cut off. Char smell.

## Aftermath *(after the run)*

### Success
Van doors open. Thermal blankets go on. Pax says Kai’s name. Sirens stay distant for now.

### Proof
A log line reads `HUMAN_SIGNATURE = PROTEIN_ANOMALY`. Someone can pocket the chip. Suture does not ask what you took.

### Failure
The van leaves light. Pax does not look at the Crew. The plant smells like copper for weeks.
