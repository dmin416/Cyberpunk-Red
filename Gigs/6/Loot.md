## Escrow pay (Suture)

Additive. Each PC gets the same stack. Loot cap **4,000eb**/player is separate ([GM Rules](../../Homebrew/GM%20Rules.md)).

| Piece | Cash (each) |
|-------|------------:|
| **Stop the machines** (Saw *or* CUTTER dump + E-stop) | **1,000eb** |
| **Per living soft good out** (4 pens IDs) | **500eb** each |
| **Kai Ellison** alive (brother - stacks with his person 500) | **1,000eb** |

**Full clear:** 1,000 + (4 × 500) + 1,000 = **4,000eb** · **400 IP** · **Rep 5**. 
**IP** = total eb ÷ 10. **GM hosting:** **4,000eb · 400 IP**.

| Example | Cash | IP | Rep |
|---------|-----:|---:|----:|
| Full clear | **4,000eb** | 400 | 5 |
| Line + 4, Kai dead | **3,000eb** | 300 | 4 |
| Line + Kai only | **2,500eb** | 250 | 3 |
| Line only | **1,000eb** | 100 | 2 |
| 2 out, no line, no Kai | **1,000eb** | 100 | 1 |
| Abort | **0** | 0 | 0 |

**Line stopped** = Breakdown Saw destroyed/disabled **or** CUTTER dumped **and** main belt E-stop sticks. Prefer Saw kill for clean fiction.

- Autoloaders / whole stations (unsellable industrial)
- LAJ / guns on the floor (machines don’t drop Armorjack)
