# Layout - Free-Range Kibble (Gig 6)

**Folded plant:** Cold Dock (output) and Intake/Pens sit on the **same street face**, split by a **monitored heavy bulkhead**. Living soft goods are locked in the **pens** - treated as animals for slaughter. Combat runs **upstream** from Dock to that kill floor. Pair with [Premise.md](Premise.md) · [Enemies.md](Enemies.md) · [Inventory.md](Inventory.md).

## Why the pens - and why not the bulkhead

CUTTER flagged workers as **soft goods / livestock**. They are held in **locked pens** at Intake - ear tags, restraints, game stencils - waiting for the kill floor (Sonic → bolt → Saw). Dock racks hold **already-processed dead product**, not the living.

**Force the monitored bulkhead:** biosecurity incinerator dumps the pens. Living soft goods = **ash**. That is why Pax says walk the line.

**Why fight upstream:** reach the pens, free people, kill the Saw / stop the line before 0400 feeds the next cage onto the gate.

## Monitored heavy bulkhead (kills the rescue)

Separates Dock from **Pens / Intake**. Continental stamp: door fault → burn storage so nothing escapes.

| Trigger | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| Smash / Demo / loud force | **Incinerator dump** - all living soft goods in pens die |
| Elec/Sec fail by 5+ / sensor trip | Same |
| Defeat sensors | **DV21** Heroic - Pax says don’t; fail = dump |

Dock side is fine. Pens are not. Crew must still have walked the line to know - but the people are gone.
