# Flavor — Gig 1

Bar slaughter in Kabuki. Pair with [Layout.md](Layout.md), [Enemies.md](Enemies.md), [Friends.md](Friends.md).

---

## Where

**Kabuki** — a Claw-friendly bar off the main neon (not the Afterlife; not fancy). Half-masks drink here. Kimen-Gumi money keeps the private room booked.

Threat: **Moderate** with Combat manners. NCPD is a punchline. Trauma shows if someone paid.

---

## Appearance

**Interior:** Low light, red lanterns, sticky floor, pachinko bleed from next door. Oni masks on the wall that aren't decorative. Private room door in the back — nicer wood, worse people.

**Street:** Wet ferrocrete, parked junkers, neon in the puddles. When the fight spills out, Kabuki watches from doorways and pretends not to.

---

## Hook

No fixer. Marshal and Weaver are drinking. Claws are loud. Marshal's chrome is louder in his head.

**Boxed open (optional):**

> The bar smells like sake, gun oil, and someone else's victory. Half-masks at the booth are laughing like Kabuki owes them rent. Weaver's watching your hands. You stand up. The laugh dies mid-breath. There was never going to be a conversation.

## Conclusion

**Resolution:** Bodies on the sticky floor / street. No contractor payout. Strip what you can before phones light up.

### Narration

- The bar is a wreck. Neon still buzzes over a booth that used to be people.
- Wave 1 iron is street Claw — good enough. If the private room emptied, the Excellent rifle and the captain's kit are the real take.
- Outside, Kabuki is already telling a story: full-borg and a Tech painted a Claw bar. That story has legs.
