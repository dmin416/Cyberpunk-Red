# Flavor — Gig 1

Bar slaughter in Kabuki. Pair with [Layout.md](Layout.md), [Enemies.md](Enemies.md), [Friends.md](Friends.md).

---

## Where

**Kabuki** — a Claw-friendly bar off the main neon (not the Afterlife; not fancy). Half-masks drink here. Kimen-Gumi money keeps the private room booked.

Threat: **Moderate** with Combat manners. NCPD is a punchline. Trauma shows if someone paid.

---

## Appearance

**Interior:** Low light, red paper lanterns, sticky floor, pachinko bleed through the wall. Oni masks that aren't décor. Private room door in back — nicer wood, worse people. Feels like a Kabuki **izakaya** that never got a Yum Seng seafood budget.

**Street:** Wet ferrocrete, neon in the puddles. **2–3 CHOOH² Roadbikes** at the curb (Claws are known for fast bikes — CP:R). Half-mask stickers on tanks; one tiger-stripe seat cover. A dented **Compact Groundcar**. When the fight spills, Kabuki watches from doorways and pretends not to.

## Hook

No fixer. Marshal and Weaver are drinking. Claws are loud. Marshal's chrome is louder in his head.

**Boxed open (optional):**

> The bar smells like warm synth-sake, SCOP grease, and someone else's victory. Half-masks at the booth — Asia Pop jackets open over Armorjack, ochoko clinking, a beer can sweating on the table next to a cup of Smash. Weaver's watching your hands. You stand up. The laugh dies mid-breath. There was never going to be a conversation.

## Claw lines

Mix English streetslang with Japanese. Gloss in parentheses for the GM — don't read the gloss aloud.

### Wave 1 (floor)

- "The fuck you looking at, chrome?"
- 「何見てんだ、ハク？」 *(Nani miten da, haku? — What're you staring at, chrome?)*
- "Sit down before we make you spare parts—"
- 「座れ。壊すぞ。」 *(Suware. Kowasu zo. — Sit. Or we break you.)*
- (when it starts) 「半面！半面だ！」 *(Hanmen! Hanmen da! — Half-masks! Masks on!)*
- 「裏だ！裏の部屋！」 *(Ura da! Ura no heya! — Back! The back room!)*
- (throwing sake) 「飲め、クソが—」 *(Nome, kuso ga— — Drink this, shithead—)* *ochoko* flies
- (radio / yell) 「カイト！銃！」 *(Kaito! Jū! — Kaito! The gun!)*

### Wave 2 (Ryo)

- "I was in a meeting."
- 「会議中だった。」 *(Kaigi-chū datta.)*
- "You kill my floor crew in my bar? In *Kabuki*?"
- 「歌舞伎で、俺の連中を？」(Kabuki de, ore no renchū o?)
- 「名前を言え。面に残す。」 *(Namae o ie. Men ni nokosu. — Say a name. It goes on the masks.)*
- (Facedown) 「出ていけ。さもなくば。」 *(Dete ike. Samonakuba. — Walk out. Or else.)*
- (last breath) 「健二が…聞く…」 *(Kenji ga… kiku… — Kenji will… hear…)*

## Conclusion

**Resolution:** Bodies on the sticky floor / street. No contractor payout. Strip what you can before phones light up.

### Narration

- The bar is a wreck. Neon still buzzes over a booth that used to be people.
- Wave 1 iron is street Claw — good enough. If the private room emptied, the Excellent rifle and the captain's kit are the real take.
- Outside, Kabuki is already telling a story: full-borg and a Tech painted a Claw bar. That story has legs.
