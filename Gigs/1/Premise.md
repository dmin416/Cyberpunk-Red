## Premise

Marshal walks into Tyger Claw territory alone.

A Kimen-Gumi-adjacent cell in **Kabuki** has been skimming protection money off a block Marshal is clearing for his **FBC rebuild debt**. The Claws know someone is coming — they do not know it is one chrome-heavy Solo in an Omega frame. Marshal's job is simple: hit the stash flat above a pachinko parlor on **Sakura Street**, recover what he is owed, and walk out before the neighborhood turns into a parade of red masks.

No backup. Bringing a crew would read as a declaration of war against the Nakagawa sphere. One FBC body, one Militech Viper, one exit route.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Solo 5** — Combat Awareness, autofire, suppression, threat prioritization
- **FBC loadout** — Omega ILF, Heavy Subdermal Plating, perfected hydraulics; tanking and closing distance
- **Athletics / Brawling** — rooftop exfil, melee in tight corridors if blades close
- **Stealth** (optional) — stairwell approach before the ambush lights up
- **Resist Torture / Drugs** — Tyger Claw combat drugs and intimidation

## Suggested Opposition

*Lethal for a **combat-focused FBC Solo 5** — **Hardened** Tyger Claws (+2 attacking/defending Skills, **−1** armor penalty). All four present from the jump; no reinforcements.*

| NPC | Count | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **Hardened Tyger Claw Lieutenant** (Hanmen squad lead) | 1 | Anchor; **Wakizashi** + **Assault Rifle**; **armorjack** (+1 SP); Combat Awareness; holds the exfil stairwell |
| **Hardened Tyger Claw Mook** | 3 | **SMG** or **Medium Melee** + **Heavy Pistol**; **Expensive** armor; two flank the flat, one covers the stairwell |

**Opening:** Lieutenant and one Mook in the main room; two Mooks in the stairwell ambush once Marshal commits.

**Terrain:** Narrow stairwell into the flat; low light; cluttered back rooms favoring blade work; one rooftop escape if Marshal wins fast enough.

**Twist:** The Lieutenant carries a **shard of the skim ledger** — proof for Marshal's contractor angle, but looting the body adds time before the stairwell Mooks collapse the fight on him.
