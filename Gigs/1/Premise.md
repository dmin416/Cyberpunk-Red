# Gig 1

**Contractor:** Marshal (personal)

**Rating:** NSFW

**Focus:** Combat

**Gig Difficulty:** Lethal (Street)

**Player Count:** 1

**Payout:** Loot equal to Lethal Street gig cash value · 300 IP · 4 rep

**Type:** Solo intervention / gang confrontation

## Premise

Marshal is already slipping.

In the early stages of **cyberpsychosis**, he is running too hot, too detached, and too ready to read every threat as permission to escalate. While moving alone through **Kabuki**, Marshal catches a Tyger Claw snatch crew forcing a woman off the street and into a stash flat above a pachinko parlor on **Sakura Street**. He was not hired for this. There is no fixer, no briefing, and no clean incentive beyond the fact that he sees it happening and decides he is not letting them take her.

That choice turns the night into a personal bloodbath. Marshal goes in alone, hits the flat before the woman disappears deeper into Tyger Claw territory, and has to decide whether he can still tell the difference between rescue and indulgence once the shooting starts.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Solo 5** — Combat Awareness, suppression, target priority, committing fast
- **FBC loadout** — Omega ILF, Heavy Subdermal Plating, hydraulics; breach and overwhelm
- **Athletics / Brawling** — close-quarters violence, stairwell control, forced movement
- **Perception / Human Perception** — clock the abduction, identify who is actually a threat, avoid killing the victim by mistake
- **Resist Torture / Drugs** — stay functional while spiraling and under pressure

## Suggested Opposition

*Lethal for a **combat-focused FBC Solo 5** — **Hardened** Tyger Claws (+2 attacking/defending Skills, **−1** armor penalty). Four on site; no reinforcements if Marshal resolves it fast.*

| NPC | Count | Notes |
|-----|-------|-------|
| **Hardened Tyger Claw Lieutenant** (Hanmen squad lead) | 1 | Anchor; **Wakizashi** + **Assault Rifle**; **armorjack** (+1 SP); Combat Awareness; controls the victim and the room tempo |
| **Hardened Tyger Claw Mook** | 3 | **SMG** or **Medium Melee** + **Heavy Pistol**; **Expensive** armor; one with the Lieutenant, two moving security and containment |

**Opening:** Marshal catches the grab in progress. Lieutenant and one Mook are already inside the flat with the woman; two Mooks hold the stairwell and hall once Marshal commits.

**Terrain:** Narrow stairwell into the flat; low light; cluttered back rooms favoring blade work; one frightened civilian in the middle of the kill zone.

**Twist:** There is **no cash reward** waiting at the end. Marshal gets whatever he can pull out of the scene: gang cash, weapons, cyberware, drugs, and valuables, including whatever is in the Claws' pockets, with total loot value roughly matching a **Lethal Street** gig payout. The real pressure is whether saving the woman costs him the cleaner tactical choices his condition is pushing him toward.

## Timezone / Availability

Solo session; combat-forward. Marshal player + GM.
