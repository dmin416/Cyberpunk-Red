**Contractor:** None (personal / impulse)

**Rating:** NSFW

**Focus:** Combat

**Payout:** Loot only — up to **5,000eb** salvage (Lethal Street cash band) · **300 IP** · **4 rep** (both waves cleared)

**Type:** Bar brawl / street slaughter

## Premise

Marshal is already slipping — early **cyberpsychosis**, running hot. He's drinking in a Kabuki bar with **Weaver**, a combat-focused Tech who knows better than to leave a chromed Solo alone with his thoughts.

Tyger Claws are in the room. Half-masks at the bar, loud, territorial, laughing like the street belongs to them. Marshal decides it doesn't. There is no contract. No fixer. He stands up and starts killing Claws.

Weaver can talk him down, back his play, or turn the bar into a killbox with Maker tricks. The fight starts inside and will spill onto the street.

**Wave 1 — floor crew:** Three **Hardened Mooks** and **one or two Hardened Lieutenants** drinking / holding the main room. This is the opening bloodbath.

**Wave 2 — private room (if Wave 1 dies easy):** Higher-ups were in a back room on Claw business. They come out when the shooting doesn't sound like a fair fight anymore — **Hardened Mini-Boss** anchor plus leftover muscle. See [Enemies.md](Enemies.md) and [Layout.md](Layout.md).

## Suggested Skillset

- **Marshal — Solo / FBC** — breach, suppress, delete priority targets
- **Weaver — Tech (Maker)** — Field Expertise mid-fight, explosives, weapon upgrades, cover / devices
- **Athletics / Evasion** — bar furniture, street cover, getting off the X
- **Perception / Tactics** — clock the private-room door before it opens
- **Resist Torture / Drugs** — Marshal staying functional while spiraling

**Twist:** No pay. Pure impulse violence. If they steamroll Wave 1, Wave 2 makes it Lethal for real. Claw heat follows either way.
