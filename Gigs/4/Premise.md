# Gig 4

**Contractor:** None (personal / impulse — same energy as Gig 1)

**GM:** —

**For:** Open signups (1–5) — likely Weaver · Exec 4 · Solo 4 · Medtech 7

**Rating:** Safe *(as long as violence isn't made overly graphic)*

**Focus:** Combat

**Gig Difficulty:** **Hazardous** to **Lethal** (Street) — **Hardened** if hardened crew joins *(scale to roster)*

**Player Count:** 1–5

**Payout:** Loot only — **~1,000eb** salvage per wave cleared · **100 IP** per wave · **1 rep** per wave (max **3 waves**)

**Type:** Bar brawl / street slaughter

## Premise

Same bad idea as [Gig 1](../1/Premise.md), different curb. The crew is eating at a **sushi stall** on a Kabuki side street — neon, steam, semi-active traffic rolling past. Tyger Claws roll up on bikes, loud and territorial. Someone stands up. There is no contract.

**Weaver** (Tech), **Exec 4**, **Solo 4**, **Medtech 7** — or whoever shows. The fight never goes indoors; it stays on the **sidewalk, stall, and traffic lanes**.

**Difficulty scales to who joins.** Baseline stat blocks in [Enemies.md](Enemies.md) are **not Hardened** (skill ceilings **11 / 13 / 15**) — fine for **Hazardous** Street at 1–3 average edgerunners. If **hardened** or combat-optimized PCs fill the table (4–5, or chromed vets), apply the [Hardened rule](../../Homebrew/GM%20Rules.md) or swap to [Gig 1](../1/Enemies.md) Hardened Claw blocks — that's **Lethal** territory.

## Combat flow

**At fight start:** roll **1d6** → that number is **Round X** when Wave 2 arrives. Write it on the map. Don't tell the table until you hear bikes.

```
[Eat at sushi stall / Claws roll up]
        │
        ▼
 WAVE 1 — Round 1
   3 Tyger Claw Mooks (street / stall)
        │
        ▼
 WAVE 2 — Start of Round X (1d6 at fight start)
   +1 Mook · +2 Lieutenants (from traffic / bikes)
        │
        ▼
 WAVE 3 — Start of Round X+1 (only if crew is still fighting)
   1 Mini-Boss ("Nori" profile — shotgun lead)
```

**Flee = no Wave 3.** If the crew breaks contact and leaves the fight, **Nori never deploys** — not at Round X+1, not as a chase. Wave 3 is only for crews still standing on the curb.

**GM tweak:** If Wave 2 enters and the crew is still fighting but folding, delay Nori to **Round X+2**. If they're steamrolling, bring her on **Round X** with Wave 2.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Solo 4** — delete priority targets, hold the line at the stall
- **Medtech 7** — keep Seriously Wounded PCs in the fight; First Aid under fire
- **Exec 4** — Facedowns, call in panic, don't get isolated in traffic
- **Weaver (Tech)** — Field gadgets, cover, stall hazards (steam, gas, propane)
- **Athletics / Evasion** — cars, curbs, bike wrecks, don't eat a compact groundcar
- **Perception / Tactics** — clock Round X bikes before they slot in behind you
