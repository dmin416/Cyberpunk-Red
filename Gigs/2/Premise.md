# Gig 2

**Contractor:** C-SWAT / NCPD (proxy hire — Max-Tac stretched thin)

**GM:** Cleric

**For:** Void

**Rating:** NSFW

**Focus:** Combat

**Gig Difficulty:** Hazardous (Street)

**Player Count:** 1+ (Void; crew optional)

**Payout:** **2,500eb** (NCPD, kill) + **500eb** (parents, child alive) = **3,000eb** full clear · **250 IP** · **5 rep** (full clear)

**Type:** Cyberpsycho takedown / hostage rescue

## Premise

A cyberpsycho has taken over a building. C-SWAT is stretched too thin to deal with it right now, so the job gets farmed out to edgerunners — Void's crew gets the call.

**Goal:** Kill the cyberpsycho (**2,500eb** from NCPD). **Also:** pull the survivor child out alive — their parents are at the cordon offering **500eb** if the kid comes back breathing.

The psycho has been holed up long enough to obsess over **traps**. Expect tripwires, improvised explosives, kill-zones, and ambush geometry — not a clean hallway shootout. Split objectives (flatline the chrome + extract the survivor) are what make this Hazardous.

A team of experienced edgerunners could struggle. High chance of injury, low chance of death. Beginners could win, but it's a gamble at best.

## Suggested Skillset

- **Solo / combat chrome** — survive the boss fight once the traps are cleared
- **Perception / Demolitions / Basic Tech** — spot and disarm traps before they delete someone
- **Athletics / Stealth** — move through a prepared kill-box without walking into every wire
- **First Aid / Medicine** — keep the survivor (and yourselves) breathing after shrapnel and SMG fire
- **Tactics** — split attention between the psycho and the rescue without feeding either objective to the other

## Conclusion

Psycho down → strip the body → first glance looks like a chrome jackpot → most of it was already ruined by overuse and neglect → **salvage caps at 3,000eb** loot (Hazardous loot limit; separate from cash). Full narration: [Flavor.md](Flavor.md#conclusion-after-the-kill).

Hand off the kid to the **parents** at the tape (and the corpse to NCPD).
