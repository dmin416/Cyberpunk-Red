### Notes

Rescue objective. Survived by hiding (Stealth 8) and knowing every quiet corner of the building (Local Expert 8). MOVE 4 — cover or carry on extract.

**Grabbed (e.g. Specter):** Contested Brawling vs **Brawling 4**. Squirm, kick, bite — will not beat chrome, but the roll covers struggle / noise.

**Under fire:** Freezes or hides; Evasion 6 if forced to dodge. One solid hit likely drops them (Death Save 2).
