## Combo — Woo gun-fu

```
[Setup] Wield Perseus (fired last Round) or Duel Caster (ROF 2+)
[Free]  Woo Technique (Gun Fu, DV15) → Gun Fu MA = weapon dmg, ≤25 m/yds, half armor, spend ammo
[Action] 2× Gun Fu MA (ROF 2)
    → optional Combat Reload (free, DV15) after a hit
```

| Step | Form | Cost | Needs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Gun Fu** | Free | ROF2 Handgun out · beat **DV15** |
| 2 | **Gun Fu MA ×2** | 1 Action | Ammo per shot · half SP · dodgeable |
| 3 | **Combat Reload** | Free | ≥1 Gun Fu hit this Turn · beat **DV15** |

**Roles:** Gun Fu = ranged MA engine · Handguns 10 = accuracy · Choy Li Fut = mobility / multi-target · Multiarm = Brawl swing / Solo CA · Drunken = unaware safety net.

## Turn plan

1. **Woo + Perseus/Duel Caster** — treat Handgun dmg as MA out to **25 m**  
2. **Shaolin Step** when you perfect-dodged — free reposition Run  
3. **Sweeping Fist** after two Choy hits — slap a second target  
4. **Armed & Dangerous** before big Brawls — favored d10 or **CA** swap  
5. **Smack Together** if holding **2+** grapples — **15** direct each  
6. **Lucky Stumble** on sneak attacks; **Joint Manipulation** when they whiff melee  
7. Dip forms only when the trigger is free and you're willing to miss on **9 + d10**
