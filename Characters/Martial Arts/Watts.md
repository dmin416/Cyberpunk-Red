# Watts

**Resolution:** `DEX + Skill + 1d10` vs **DV** (or vs defender's roll where noted). MA Attacks = ROF2, half armor.

**Role:** **Solo** · **FBC** - **6× Cyberarm** combat shell (Biosystem). **BODY 14–16** depending on frame.

**Focus order:** **Wrestling → Multiarm → Silat → Aikido** (situational).

### Wrestling — **6** *(primary)*

| Move | DV | Action | Roll |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Chokehold** | **15** | Free | Attacker in a grapple, haven't Choked this turn → **Choke** with no Action cost. Can't Choke that same target again this turn. |
| **Reversal** | **15** | Free | Defender in a **1v1** grapple (neither in another grapple) → end it and become the **attacker** on them. |

*Choke / Throw = **BODY** direct to HP, ignores armor, no ablation. Choke leaves them at 1 HP + Unconscious instead of killing past 0 if they had >1 HP; 3 successive Choke rounds = Unconscious anyway.*

## Combo — clinch pipeline *(default)*

```
[1 Action] Grab (Brawling) + Armed & Dangerous (Multiarm)
    → Violent Leverage (free, Silat) → bonus Melee (if chrome ready)
        → next turns: Chokehold (free, Wrestling) while Action = Grab #2 / Throw / gun
            → Smack Together (Action, Multiarm) when holding 2+
```

| Step | Form | Cost | Needs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Brawling** | 1 Action (Grab) | Free hand · beat their Brawling |
| 2 | **Armed & Dangerous** | Free | **6 arms** > target · before Grab/Brawling roll |
| 3 | **Violent Leverage** | Free | New grapple this combat · beat **DV15** (Silat) |
| 4 | **Chokehold** | Free | Attacker in grapple · beat **DV15** (Wrestling) → BODY direct |
| 5 | **Smack Together** | 1 Action | **2+** grapples · beat **DV15** → BODY to **all** held |

**Peak turn (2+ held):** **Chokehold** (free BODY on one) + **Smack Together** (Action BODY on everyone). Can't also spend the Action on a paid Choke.

**Roles:** Wrestling = choke / reverse · Multiarm = land grabs + multi-smash · Silat = free melee after grab · Aikido = only when guns need Iron Grip / strip.

## Combo — disarm pipeline *(situational)*

When a shooter needs the gun gone: **Silat** MA ×2 → **Internal Power** → Multiarm Brawling → **Aikido Disarming Combination**.

```
[1 Action] 2× Silat MA hits (same target)
    → Internal Power (free, DV15, Silat) → bonus Brawling
        → Armed & Dangerous (free, DV15, Multiarm) → favored d10 OR Solo CA reassign
            → if Brawling hits → Disarming Combination (free, DV15, Aikido) → steal/drop weapon
```

*Gotchas:* Internal Power and Disarming are **once/turn** each. Disarm needs **MA + Brawling** hits same turn (pure chrome ×2 Internal Power does not satisfy Aikido alone).
