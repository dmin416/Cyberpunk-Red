## Combo — disarm pipeline

One-turn opener: **Silat** converts MA hits into a boosted **Brawling** swing; **Multiarm** makes it land; **Aikido** strips the weapon.

```
[1 Action] 2× Silat MA hits (same target)
    → Internal Power (free, DV15, Silat) → bonus Brawling
        → Armed & Dangerous (free, DV15, Multiarm) → favored d10 OR Solo CA reassign
            → if Brawling hits → Disarming Combination (free, DV15, Aikido) → steal/drop weapon
                → Athletics: throw weapon away (25 m/yds) or hold in spare hand
```

| Step | Form | Cost | Needs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Silat** | 1 Action (MA Attack, ROF 2) | Both hits on **same** target |
| 2 | **Internal Power** | Free | **WILL 6+** · beat **DV15** |
| 3 | **Armed & Dangerous** | Free | **6 arms** > target · before Brawling roll · 2nd Brawling this turn = **DV17** |
| 4 | Bonus **Brawling** | Free | Attack roll (favored d10 or CA swap if step 3 succeeded) |
| 5 | **Disarming Combination** | Free | Same target · 1 MA + 1 Brawling hit this turn · beat **DV15** |

**Roles:** Silat = engine · Multiarm = accuracy / Solo CA · Aikido = disarm · **Athletics** = ditch or repurpose stolen gear.

**Gotchas:** Internal Power and Disarming are **once/turn** each. Disarm fails if bonus Brawling misses even when both Silat hits land. Each special move rolls **`DEX + that form + 1d10`**.

*Alt setup:* two **Light/Medium Melee** hits on same target also triggers Internal Power.
