## Turn plan

1. **Dodge** → **Shaolin Step** → reposition  
2. **Woo Technique** when you have the shot — primary kills  
3. **Jujutsu** — two hits on one target → **throw** (prone, damage, clinch ends)  
4. **Sweeping Fist** when it’s a scrum (two CLF hits → bonus **Brawling** on a third)  
5. **Melee Gun Fu** — **4d6 × 2** when in face without Woo this turn
