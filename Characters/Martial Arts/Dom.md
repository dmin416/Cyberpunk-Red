# Dom

**Resolution:** `DEX + Skill + 1d10` vs **DV** (or vs defender's roll where noted). MA Attacks = ROF2, half armor.

## Stats

| INT | REF | DEX | TECH | COOL | WILL | MOVE | BODY | EMP | LUCK |
| ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 8 | 2 | 8 | 2 | 8 | 8 | 8 | 12 | 6 | 8 |

**HP:** 60 *(BODY 12 + WILL 8)* · **MA damage:** **4d6** *(BODY 11+)* · **Death Save:** 12

*Melee specialist — expensive blade is primary damage. **REF 2** → initiative weak. **LUCK 8** for clutch DV rolls.*

## Defense layers

| Situation | Tool |
| --- | --- |
| Aware shot / thrown / blast, blade out | **Cut the Bullet** (Kendo) |
| Unaware attack | **Lucky Stumble** (Drunken) |
| About to be grabbed / thrown | **Deashi** (Sumo) |
| Already grabbed | Opposed **Brawling 6** |
| Facedown | **Niramiai** (Sumo) — **+2** after **DV15** |
| Act late (**REF 2**) | **Superior Stance** after a MA hit (Kung Fu) |

## Turn plan

1. **Blade out** — fight on **Melee Weapon 6**; **Ki Ken Tai** when you need a precision cut next turn  
2. **MA hit** → try **Superior Stance** (free, Kung Fu 1)  
3. **Aware** ranged threat → **Cut the Bullet**  
4. **Unaware** attack → **Lucky Stumble**; burn **Luck** if needed  
5. **Grab coming** → **Deashi** before the clinch sticks  
6. **Already grabbed** → opposed **Brawling 6**  
7. **Facedown** → **Niramiai** for **+2** (**COOL 8**)
