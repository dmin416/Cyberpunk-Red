# Hopes

Plans and gear wishlist.

## Wishlist

### Malorian Sub-Flechette Gun

**Cost:** 5,000eb (Luxury)

Excellent Quality Exotic Heavy SMG. 3d6 damage, 25-round mag (H Pistol), ROF 1, Autofire (×4), 1 hand, not concealable. Smartgun Link included; 3 attachment slots. Fires only its proprietary caseless flechette AP ammo, which **ablates armor by 4** instead of 1. Ammo is 100eb (Premium) per 10.

### Hello Cutie Hypurr-Hammer

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive)

Standard Quality Exotic Melee weapon (2 hands), 4d6 damage. Sliding ultra-dense core (four breed designs: Bengal, Egyptian Mau, Sphinx, Ussuri).

- **No half armor** (armor is not halved against it).
- **BODY 9+:** deals 5d6 and lets you pick 2 Critical Injuries.
- **BODY 8 or less:** the hammer mocks the user.

### Hello Cutie Starlight Finale (Rocket Launcher)

**Cost:** 5,000eb (Luxury)

Exotic Rocket Launcher. Fires proprietary "Finale" Rockets (500eb each) with a two-stage effect:

1. Non-damaging sensory blast in a 12yd radius (6×6). DV19 Resist Torture/Drugs or take **-2 to all Actions for two rounds**.
2. Lethal sub-munitions detonate in the same area for **8d6** damage to everyone caught in the radius.

### Heavy Subdermal Plating

**Cost:** 5,000eb (Luxury)  
**HL:** 14 (4d6) • **Install:** Hospital

Borgware. External Body / Subdermal Armor. Hard plates under the skin.

- **SP 13** on body and head
- **Armor penalty:** -2 REF, DEX, MOVE — **negated at BODY 14+ or if FBC**
- Requires **BODY 10+** unless FBC
- Nanomachines repair **1 ablated SP/day** on body and head after a full day of natural healing

### Trauma Response Nanomatrix

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive)  
**HL:** 7 (2d6) • **Install:** Hospital

Internal Body cyberware. Nanomachines that regenerate armor under the skin.

- Once per day (Action): restore **Skinweave or Subdermal Armor** to full SP
- Requires Skinweave or Subdermal Armor installed
- Each additional installation adds **+1 daily use**

### Rocklin External RacerBracer

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive)

> "For racers too heavily chromed up to install it, Rocklin presents the External RacerBracer."
>
> "First developed for Rocklin Augmentics' elite street racing team, the RacerBracer will protect your cervical spine to ensure you never suffer the humiliating experience of arriving at a race while wearing a neck brace again."

External alternative to the internal Racer Bracer (500eb, HL 7). A user wearing a Rocklin Augmentics External RacerBracer is **immune to the Whiplash Critical Injury**.

## Making It Yourself (Tech Fabrication)

*RAW: Fabrication materials cost **one price category lower** than the item; DV/time is set by the item's own category. Homebrew (Blue): Techs/Medtechs buy materials at base cost — no Fixer needed.*

| Item | Retail (Category) | Fab materials | Craft DV / DTD | Repair skill |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Malorian Sub-Flechette Gun | 5,000eb (Luxury) | **1,000eb** | DV29 / **28 DTD** | Weaponstech |
| Starlight Finale (Rocket Launcher) | 5,000eb (Luxury) | **1,000eb** | DV29 / **28 DTD** | Weaponstech |
| Heavy Subdermal Plating | 5,000eb (Luxury) | **1,000eb** | DV29 / **28 DTD** | Cybertech |
| Hello Cutie Hypurr-Hammer | 1,000eb (V. Expensive) | **500eb** | DV24 / **14 DTD** | Weaponstech |
| Trauma Response Nanomatrix | 1,000eb (V. Expensive) | **500eb** | DV24 / **14 DTD** | Cybertech |
| Rocklin External RacerBracer | 1,000eb (V. Expensive) | **500eb** | DV24 / **14 DTD** | Cybertech |

**Materials total ≈ 4,500eb** vs. **14,000eb** retail — roughly **68% off**.

**Total downtime:** 3×28 + 3×14 = **126 DTD** to fab everything.

**With a Tech HQ (workstation):** total ÷ 3 = **42 DTD**.

**Ammo (optional fab):** Sub-Flechette AP ammo 100eb (Premium) → **50eb / 10** (DV17, **1 DTD**); Finale Rockets 500eb (Expensive) → **100eb each** (DV21, **7 DTD** each).

*(DTD conversion: 1 day = 1 DTD — Premium 1 / Expensive 7 / V. Expensive 14 / Luxury 28.)*

**Caveats:**

- Fabrication only *builds* the item. The three **cyberware** items (Heavy Subdermal, Trauma Nanomatrix — plus meat-body **BODY gates**) still need a **Medtech Hospital install** and cost **Humanity Loss**. Weapons just need fabrication.
- Excellent Quality weapons (Sub-Flechette) can also be made by fabricating an Average weapon then applying an **Upgrade Expertise** bump instead.
