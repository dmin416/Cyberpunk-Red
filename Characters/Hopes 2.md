# Hopes 2

Plans and gear wishlist — **FBC** (Full Body Conversion) build.

## FBC Requirements

*Source: Interface RED Vol 3 — Going Metal*

An FBC is a cybernetic body built around an **Internal Linear Frame**, into which a **Biosystem** (your brain + identity) is installed. **Permanent** — you lose your meat body forever. Biosystem cost and HL are **separate** from the FBC body package.

### Before Surgery (typical surgeon requirements)

1. **Remove all non-medical cyberware** — including Neuralware (uninstalled and returned, bagged).
2. **Undergo therapy** — prepare for massive Humanity Loss; can raise Humanity before going under.
3. Optional: **BD therapy** between Biosystem install and body implant to restore max Humanity before package HL hits.

### Step 1 — Biosystem

**Cost:** 10,000eb (Super Luxury) • **Install:** Hospital  
**HL:** 14 (4d6)

- Brain and identity moved into a Biopod; meat body is returned (decomposes unless cryo-stored).
- Cannot install cyberware directly while in a Biosystem — only through an FBC body.
- Outside a body: Cover 25 HP shell; brain dies on direct hit; observation/communication only.
- **Monthly:** Glucose-Rich Brain Solution (100eb Premium) via access port — free with Good Prepack lifestyle (600eb/month).

### Step 2 — FBC Body Package

**Cost:** varies by model (see table)  
**Install:** Hospital (requires Biosystem) — **included at no extra cost**  
**Package HL:** varies (roll or take average on install)

You can either **buy a branded body** (below) or **build one from parts** as a Tech.

### What FBC Status Gives You (relevant to this list)

- **Bypasses BODY gates** on Borgware like **Dragoon Plating** (no BODY 16 required).
- **Counts as having Grafted Muscle and Bone Lace** for any cyberware that requires it.
- **Double option slots** on foundational + Internal/External Body cyberware.
- Baseline cyberware (arms, legs, skull, ILF, Neural Link) installs at **0 HL** in the body.
- Immune to many crits, wound-state penalties, disease, poison, radiation; no food/water/breath needed.
- **Lifestyle:** Good Prepack (600eb/month) or better for daily HP regen and self-repair (DV15 Cybertech, 4 hr).

### Body Transfer (later)

Move Biosystem to a new FBC body: **DV17** Cybertech or Surgery, **4 hours**, reattemptable. New cyberware on the body causes fresh HL.

## Wishlist

### Dragoon Plating

**Cost:** 5,000eb (Luxury)  
**HL:** 14 (4d6) • **Install:** Hospital

Borgware. External Body cyberware. Magnetically seated armor panels on body hardpoints — hot-swappable mid-fight.

- **SP 15** on body and head
- **Armor penalty:** -2 REF, DEX, MOVE
- Requires **BODY 16+** unless **FBC** (this build is FBC — no BODY gate)
- **Jettison** (Action): drop plating, lose SP and penalty
- **Magnetic recall** (Action): summon unattached set within 6m/yds; it flies to you and attaches
- Nanomachines repair **1 ablated SP/day** when no SP was lost that day
- Extra sets: **1,000eb** (Very Expensive); custom colors **+100eb**

### Rocklin Augmentics Skydrivers

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive)  
**HL:** 14 (4d6) • **Install:** Hospital

Rocklin's paired specialty cyberlegs — designed by guerrilla artist Aventó Ar/gentium. *"Nothing stops traffic like a great set of legs."*

- **Paired Cyberlegs** — single purchase installs both; one HL roll for the pair
- Each leg: **2 Option Slots** (Cyberleg / Cyberlimb options)
- **Jump Boosters** pre-installed on each leg (0 HL, no slot cost; **cannot be removed** without destroying the legs)
- After moving **at least 4 m/yds** this turn, Brawling or Martial Arts kicks against **cover only** gain **+3d6** damage
- Each leg still counts separately for Critical Injuries and repair

*Replaces standard FBC cyberlegs — not included in body package at 0 HL.*

### FBC Gemini Sculpt

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive)  
**HL:** 0 (N/A) • **Install:** Hospital • **FBC only**

Raven Microcybernetics external body shell — reformats the FBC to baseline human shape and wraps it in **Realskin™**.

- **Indistinguishable from a biological human by sight** — IR reveals cold metal (no body heat)
- **Not compatible** with Internal Linear Frame **Omega** or **BODY 16+** FBCs (too massive for the shell)
- Rumors exist of **Dragoon**-sized builds fitting via Tech-upgraded Gemini Sculpts — **unsubstantiated**; confirm with GM/Tech before banking on Dragoon + Sculpt together

### Compact Framing

**Cost:** 1,000eb (Very Expensive) — *server-approved invention (Kat)*  
**Type:** Internal Linear Frame **Sigma** upgrade

> *"Relax gonk, even the Inquisitors won't be able to tell."*

Lighter materials + split motors — no expansion points needed.

- Makes the Sigma frame **fully concealed**
- Negates ILF Sigma **height** increase (flavor only)
- Negates ILF Sigma **weight** increase — use gear/options that raw Sigma weight would block

**Install (homebrew):** Tech crafts the upgrade (**14 DTD**, **DV24** upgrade/invention check) → Medtech installs (**DV17** Surgery, **4 hours** DTD). Failed surgery loses downtime only; upgrade item is **not** consumed on failure.

*Requires a **Sigma**-frame FBC body — pick body model accordingly.*
