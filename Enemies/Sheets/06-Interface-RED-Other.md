## Georgia on My Mind

IR5 Solo of Fortune

*Basement*

A Mercenary Level Screamsheet Mission

#### Player Information

SovOil is looking to hire a team of mercenaries to do some dirty work. They want to kidnap the daughter of a leader of Petrochem-backed Georgian rebels to encourage them to cease their attacks on SovOil holdings in the oil-rich American South. The megacorp will hire anyone to do the delicate job, no matter how ill suited they may be. It does not care for a millisecond about the safety of the woman they plan on capturing, and this disregard is evident. SovOil plans on hiring separate teams to capture other children and loved ones of the remaining rebel leaders simultaneously. The megacorp's money, however, is good, and the job, however immoral, seems simple enough on face value. The job pays 5,000eb per person, and comes with an operational budget of 5,000eb per person. All just to kidnap one little girl, home alone.

Any team that accepts the job will receive this briefing from a ski-masked SovOil Deniable Operations Chief that introduces himself as "Revmir Babich", obviously not his real name.

"Your job is to extract one "Kelly McAlister" from her family's fortified compound in the Panola Mountain wilderness. This job is part of a series of simultaneous operations in the area. It is possible that she will know you are coming, but we have it on good authority that she'll be completely alone on the property. We'll transport you and your vehicles to Atlanta by ocean, where we've set up an operations base on the fourth and fifth floor of the Wright Hotel. You'll have 48 hours to acclimatize before the operation begins. If you encounter local resistance or law enforcement, you are on your own. You will not receive backup and SovOil will not claim you. Your team will be paid upon the delivery of Kelly, alive, to the Wright Hotel. If she dies, you'll receive no payment. Either way, we'll transport you back. But let's not kill the kid, yeah? That's not what anyone wants here."

#### Gamemaster Notes

The Wright Hotel is a Headquarters with 16 beds, Morale Boost 4, Upgraded Lockup (6 cells), Upgraded Medbay & a Workstation.

Kelly is indeed home alone, but is by no means undefended. SovOil's intelligence on this operation is surface level and missing the mark on several important ideas. Any investigation-minded Edgerunner can find this information with relative ease (DV15).

For one, Kelly is 32, not exactly a little girl, and has two masters degrees: one in robotics and the other in rare earth mining engineering. SovOil has done only a cursory search, returning the only images that can be found of her on the Data Pool — the ones from the various robotics fairs she won in high school. Her name appears on two patents for mining robots:

1. **The Rock Razer:** An Efreet-piloted Ground Drone w/ EQ Very Heavy Melee Weapon, Popup Shield, Observation Camera, and 11 SP.
2. **Mineral Softener:** An Efreet-piloted Large Air Drone w/ EQ Flamethrower, Grenade Launcher w/ Incendiary Grenades, and 11 SP.

Kelly is a private person, and not much else can be found on her, unless someone digs deep (DV24), in which case they discover that after a recent series of exotic surgeries she appears crocodilian. This is the reason Kelly is currently on her own — most of her friends and family are wary of the bodysculpt and do not want to be near her right now.

Kelly's father, Irvine McAlister, is the financial brain that stretches every dollar his Georgian Rebel group receives from Petrochem. While he doesn't understand his daughter's "attitude issues," he is proud of her scientific accomplishments, loves her, and stands up for her when she's called "cold blooded," at family gatherings. He also loves rolling up his sleeves and doing renovation projects with her around the house.

The McAlister Ranch is located on 10 acres in the Panola Mountain wilderness. It consists of a main house and a guest house, both of which border a small man-made fishing lake.

##### Main House

The main house has two floors and a basement.

**1st Floor Rooms** (defenses in parenthesis)

A beautiful wraparound Porch (Laser Grid), Bathroom (Stun Panels), Den (Stun Panels), Dining Room (Tip Floor), Kitchen (Wall Punchers), Laundry Room.

**2nd Floor Rooms** (defenses in parenthesis)

A stately Balcony overlooking the entrance, Main Bathroom (Goop), Two Bedrooms, Main Bedroom, Walk-in Closet (Ceiling Punchers) with a chute to the basement.

**Basement**

The basement is a robot workshop and armory, contains the ranch's NET Architecture. It connects to the bomb shelter via a secret passage (DV15 to locate).

##### Guest House

The guest house has a single floor and a basement.

**1st Floor Rooms** (defenses in parenthesis)

Bathroom, Bedroom (Stun Panels), Billiards Room (Electrical Flooring), Small Kitchen (Goop).

**Basement**

The basement is empty and connects to the bomb shelter via a secret passage (DV15 to locate).

##### Bomb Shelter

The bomb shelter underground has been converted into a two lane bowling alley as part of Irvine's hobby project. It is guarded by 2 Automated Turrets that fire bowling balls (5d6 damage). It also has a secret door (DV17 to locate) leading to a flooded tunnel that opens out in the lake.

##### Begin Operation

When the operation begins at sundown on the third day, Kelly is aware the Crew is coming, and has her defenses set up. She knows that if she can survive until morning she will be saved by Petrochem-backed mercenaries (a Rival Crew). She also has the following at her disposal:

- A number of Rock Razer drones equal to the number of Edgerunners.
- A number of Mineral Softener drones equal to the number of Edgerunners. Each drone carries its own NET Architecture.
- Four Thermal Decoys in the shape of Rock Razer drones set up at various points on the property.

Should the Crew betray their employer at any point, SovOil will send two waves of Rival Crews to dispatch both them and Kelly, and Petrochem's incoming crew (sent to rescue Kelly) will be dispatched off-camera. In this case, Petrochem will end up paying out the Crew what SovOil would have if Kelly survives.
