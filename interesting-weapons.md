# Interesting Weapons

## Militech Perseus

- **Price:** 5,000eb (Luxury)
- **Why:** Excellent VH pistol (4d6). Base ROF 1, but becomes ROF 2 if you fired it last Round — sustained fire without dropping to a lesser weapon class. Forearm brace + compensators.

## Malorian Sub-Flechette Gun

- **Price:** 5,000eb (Luxury)
- **Why:** Excellent Exotic Heavy SMG. Autofire (×4) with proprietary flechette AP that ablates armor by **4** per hit (not 1). Smartgun Link included. Proprietary ammo only.

## Con Arms Multi Ammo Pistol

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic VH pistol (4d6). Load up to 5 different VH ammo types in the 5-round mag and pick which type each shot — AP, expansive, incendiary, etc. on demand without swapping guns.

## Hello Cutie Janus Hex

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Excellent concealable “pistol” that fires scent balls (1d6). Hit → DV15 Resist Torture/Drugs or −3 Ranged/−1 Melee and vision Perception for 1 hour (or 10 min flush). Debuff gun, not a damage weapon. Ammo 20eb/4.

## Magnum Opus Hellbringer

- **Price:** 1,000eb (V Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic VH pistol with **5d6** (same punch as a Malorian-class shot). Break-action revolver; jams after every shot if BODY under 10 (Action to clear). Glass cannon for high-BODY shooters.

## Hello Cutie ModBall Gun

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Concealable utility “pistol” — no bullet damage. 5-round mag; mix ≤5 ModBall types and pick per shot (same trick as Multi Ammo). Construction Kit 100eb crafts balls as Fab Expertise 2 (1 hr each).

## PS E-TACK Rapid Responder

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Poor Medium pistol that bursts: 3 rounds/trigger → **3d6 as Heavy Pistol**, and non-AP ammo becomes AP. Extended Mag (18) + Stun Bayonet (Stun Baton rules) included. Burst needs ≥3 rounds in mag. Cheap punch + melee option, pays for it with Poor jam.

## Fire Extinguisher

- **Price:** 50eb (Costly)
- **Why:** Exotic “VH pistol” — foam, not bullets. Max 6 m/yds; hit ends On Fire. Foam damages fire objects with HP (extinguishes at 0). 5 charges; recharge 20eb or 5 min at a station. Utility tool that still uses Handgun.

## E-TACK Public Defender

- **Price:** 50eb (Costly)
- **Why:** Exotic Poor Heavy pistol — no trigger; Smartgun Link required (included), Subdermal Grip only. Per shot: lethal **3d6** or less-lethal (Stun Gun rules, drains battery). Dirt-cheap dual-mode sidearm if you’re already wired for smartguns.

## Duel Caster

- **Price:** 100eb (Premium)
- **Why:** Exotic card-launcher (1d6). ROF 3 alone / 2 otherwise; spends **3 Attack Checks per Action** (breaks the usual 2-AC rule). ELO TCG cards recoverable; cheap playing cards work but as Poor Quality. Weird high-volume gimmick gun.

## Nomad Rocker

- **Price:** 100eb (Premium)
- **Why:** Exotic Poor VH pistol (4d6) that fires **rocks** — free ammo forever. Destroyed → repair with no Check (1 hr); rinse by dropping in water and firing.

## Microwaver

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic EMP “pistol.” Hit → DV15 Cybertech or **2** cyberware/electronics inoperable for 1 min (limbs hang limp). Battery pack, 8 shots.

## Militech Crusher

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic VH-frame handgun that only fires shotgun shells — **5d6 slug** in a one-handed package. Shotgun punch without a shoulder arm.

## Shrieker

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic sonic pistol. Hit → DV15 Resist Torture/Drugs or Damaged Ear. Shooter needs ear protection or takes Damaged Ear when firing. Battery pack, 8 shots.

## Militech Tactical Umbrella

- **Price:** 1,000eb (V Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic combo — **Excellent Heavy melee** (3d6) + **Poor Heavy pistol** (3d6, 2 rnd). Acid/Blood Rain protection while held; Non-Basic ammo ok. One hand locked to the umbrella.

## Pursuit Security Bouncer

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Why:** Exotic glove — Stun Baton melee (2d6) + Microwaver EMP in one wearable. Wear/remove is an Action; blocks most Cyberhand options while on; dead battery kills both modes.
