# Interesting Weapons

## Militech Perseus

- **Price:** 5,000eb (Luxury)
- **Damage:** 4d6
- **ROF:** 1 (2 if fired last Round)
- **Source:** BC 112
- **Why:** Excellent VH pistol. Sustained fire without dropping to a lesser weapon class. Forearm brace + compensators.

## Malorian Sub-Flechette Gun

- **Price:** 5,000eb (Luxury)
- **Damage:** 3d6 (Autofire ×4)
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** DL:12Gun 4, IR2 60, IR5 143
- **Why:** Excellent Exotic Heavy SMG. Proprietary flechette AP that ablates armor by **4** per hit (not 1). Smartgun Link included. Proprietary ammo only.

## Con Arms Multi Ammo Pistol

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** 4d6
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** DL:12Gun 3, DGD 144
- **Why:** Exotic VH pistol. Load up to 5 different VH ammo types in the 5-round mag and pick which type each shot — AP, expansive, incendiary, etc. on demand without swapping guns.

## Magnum Opus Hellbringer

- **Price:** 1,000eb (V Expensive)
- **Damage:** 5d6
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** DL:12Gun 4, DGD 144
- **Why:** Exotic VH pistol (same punch as a Malorian-class shot). Break-action revolver; jams after every shot if BODY under 10 (Action to clear). Glass cannon for high-BODY shooters.

## Hello Cutie ModBall Gun

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** — (by ModBall)
- **ROF:** 2
- **Source:** DL:12Cute 5
- **Why:** Concealable utility “pistol” — no bullet damage. 5-round mag; mix ≤5 ModBall types and pick per shot (same trick as Multi Ammo). Construction Kit 100eb crafts balls as Fab Expertise 2 (1 hr each).

## E-TACK Public Defender

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** 3d6 (lethal or less-lethal)
- **ROF:** 2
- **Source:** BC 115
- **Why:** Exotic Excellent Heavy pistol — no trigger; Smartgun Link required (included), Subdermal Grip only. Per shot: lethal or less-lethal (Stun Gun rules, drains battery). Dual-mode sidearm if you’re already wired for smartguns.

## Duel Caster

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** 1d6
- **ROF:** 3 alone / 2 otherwise
- **Source:** DL:ElfTCG 9, IR3 73
- **Why:** Exotic Excellent card-launcher. Spends **3 Attack Checks per Action** (breaks the usual 2-AC rule). ELO TCG cards recoverable; cheap playing cards work but as Poor Quality. Weird high-volume gimmick gun.

## Militech Crusher

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** 3d6 (shell cone)
- **ROF:** 1
- **Mag:** 6 (Shell)
- **Source:** DL:12Gun 4, DGD 144
- **Why:** Exotic VH-frame handgun that only fires **Basic Shotgun Shell** — cone (DV13, 6 m, no Aimed Shot). Room-sweep tool, not a slug cannon.

## Backup

### Fire Extinguisher

- **Price:** 50eb (Costly)
- **Damage:** — (ends On Fire; damages fire HP)
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** T:Hope 34
- **Why:** Exotic “VH pistol” — foam, not bullets. Max 6 m/yds. Foam damages fire objects with HP (extinguishes at 0). 5 charges; recharge 20eb or 5 min at a station. Utility tool that still uses Handgun.

### Microwaver

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** — (EMP)
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** CP:R 348
- **Why:** Exotic EMP “pistol.” Hit → DV15 Cybertech or **2** cyberware/electronics inoperable for 1 min (limbs hang limp). Battery pack, 8 shots.

### Shrieker

- **Price:** 500eb (Expensive)
- **Damage:** — (Damaged Ear)
- **ROF:** 1
- **Source:** CP:R 349
- **Why:** Exotic sonic pistol. Hit → DV15 Resist Torture/Drugs or Damaged Ear. Shooter needs ear protection or takes Damaged Ear when firing. Battery pack, 8 shots.
