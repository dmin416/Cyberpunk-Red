# Rules

Campaign and house rules for Epics.

## Downtime

- **1 month = 30 DTD** (downtime days).

## Salvaging

- Salvaging is a **daily** activity (**1 DTD**), not a week-long one.

## Surge

- You can take **one Surge per week**.

## Skill rolls

When asked to roll a skill, **roll in bash** — run real `d10` commands in the shell. Do not simulate or invent results.

1. Roll **1d10** and add the relevant **stat** and **skill**.
2. **Explosion** (once per roll only):
   - On a **1**: add that 1 to the total, then **subtract** another d10 roll.
   - On a **10**: add that 10 to the total, then **add** another d10 roll.
3. The second d10 from explosion does **not** trigger another explosion, even if it is a 1 or 10.

## Character stats

When updating **any stat** on a player file (HP, Humanity, EMP, BODY, etc.), add a row to that character's **Stat changes** table with **was → now** and **why**. Do not change stats silently.
