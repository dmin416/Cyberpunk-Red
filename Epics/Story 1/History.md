# History

## January 2040

- Wade "Watts" Wotzs enters the story as a Tech raised by corporate technicians, known for an optimistic attitude, a cluttered-but-organized workspace, and a reputation for building gear for Solos and mercs.
- Watts' family is under debt pressure after his parents missed bills and were forced to work for creditors, giving him extra reason to keep business flowing and stay useful.
- Darius "Cleric" Stone enters the story as a former corporate child turned street Medtech, a detached surgeon-therapist whose obsession with knowledge survived his family's fall from executive privilege.
- Cleric works alongside Watts, bringing clinical precision, a sterile workspace, and a useful NCPD connection into the crew's orbit.
- Luminx enters the story as a solo Rockerboy with a sharp wit, a carefully curated Asia Pop image, and ambitions built around fear, respect, and performance.
- Her corporate family background is tangled in a longer conspiracy, giving her both a taste for status and a personal reason to strike back through fame and influence.
- Dom enters the story as a Rocklin Augmentics sales Exec, pragmatic and ambitious, with one foot on the corporate ladder and the other pointed toward independence.
- His company housing and good standing with his boss give him stability, but his family's collapse into intrigue and regret keeps power and self-determination at the center of his goals.
