## Role Ability

**Maker Rank:** 4

**Specialty Ranks (8):** Gained 2 per Maker Rank (Ranks 1–4). Allocate among Field Expertise, Upgrade Expertise, Fabrication Expertise, and Invention Expertise.

### Rank 4 Benefits

- **8 total Maker specialty ranks** to spread across four specialties.
- **Field Expertise** — add specialty Rank to Basic Tech, Cybertech, Electronics/Security Tech, Weaponstech, and vehicle Tech Checks (non-Maker use). Jury-rig destroyed gear to full condition as an Action; holds 10 min per Rank, then reverts (can't jury-rig again until fully repaired).
- **Upgrade Expertise** — permanently improve items (1 upgrade per item): lower cyberware Humanity Loss, +1 slot, halve repair time, conceal 1H weapon, Average→Excellent weapon, Exotic attachment slot, Exotic non-basic ammo, +1 SP, Nomad Rank-1 vehicle upgrade, or install invented upgrades.
- **Fabrication Expertise** — craft existing or invented items from materials one price tier below item cost (half cost for Super Luxury).
- **Invention Expertise** — design new items/upgrades with GM approval; prototype via Fabrication or Upgrade.
- **Lifestyle** — treated as one Lifestyle tier higher for maintaining gear you own.
