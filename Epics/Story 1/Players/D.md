**Combat Awareness Rank:** 4

|---------|------|--------|
| **Damage Deflection** | 2 / 4 / 6 / 8 / 10 | Reduce first damage taken this Round by 1 / 2 / 3 / 4 / 5 |
| **Fumble Recovery** | 4 | Ignore critical failures (1s) on attack rolls (still count as 1) |
| **Initiative Reaction** | 1 per | +1 to Initiative rolls |
| **Precision Attack** | 3 / 6 / 9 | +1 / +2 / +3 to Attacks |
| **Spot Weakness** | 1 per | +1 damage (before armor) on first successful Attack each Round |
| **Threat Detection** | 1 per | +1 to Perception Checks |
| **Lifestyle** | — | Treated as one Lifestyle tier higher for maintaining weapons and armor |
