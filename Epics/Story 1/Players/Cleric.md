## Role Ability

**Medicine Rank:** 4

**Specialty Points (4):** Allocate among Surgery, Medical Tech (Pharmaceuticals), and Medical Tech (Cryosystem Operation).

### Rank 4 Benefits

- **4 Medicine specialty points** — each Rank gained grants 1 point to a specialty of your choice.
- **Surgery access** — install cyberware, treat severe Critical Injuries, bodysculpting, and Therapy (Medtech-only).
- **Pharmaceuticals** — synthesize medical drugs with Medical Tech (DV13); doses per hour = Medical Tech Skill from 200eb materials.
- **Cryosystem** — operate cryo gear; Cryopump at 1+ points in this specialty (see rank table below).

### Specialty Options

| Specialty | Per Point | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **Surgery** | +2 Surgery Skill (max 10) | Severe crits, cyberware install |
| **Pharmaceuticals** | +1 Medical Tech (max 5 in specialty) | Unlocks 1 pharmaceutical per point: Antibiotic, Rapidetox, Speedheal, Stim, Surge |
| **Cryosystem** | +1 Medical Tech (max 5 in specialty) | 1 pt: Cryopump · 2 pt: Registered Cryotank Tech · 3 pt: personal Cryotank · 4 pt: +2 Cryotanks, Cryopump 2 charges/2 bodies · 5 pt: +3 Cryotanks, Cryopump 3 charges/3 bodies |
