# Source Text Index

Extracted and planned text files for each publication. PDFs live in [`PDF/`](../PDF/). **All 15 books have extracted section files** (Core Rulebook: 18 chapters; other books: section files per `Index.md`).

## Books

- [Cyberpunk RED Core Rulebook](<Core Rulebook/Index.md>) — The full game.
- [Black Chrome](<Black Chrome/Index.md>) — Gear and equipment supplement.
- [Danger Gal Dossier](<Danger Gal Dossier/Index.md>) — The essential Night City factions and NPC sourcebook with over 600 locations, 99 stat blocks, mission framework, and faction dossiers.
- [Danger Gal Dossier+](<Danger Gal Dossier+/Index.md>) — Free cut content from Danger Gal Dossier: faction plot hooks, a fourth Pinny Arcade jacket, extra Incident mission information, and a bonus Bozo NPC.
- [Tales of the RED: Street Stories](<Tales of the RED Street Stories/Index.md>) — A collection of nine never-before-seen full-length missions.
- [Tales of the RED: Hope Reborn](<Tales of the RED Hope Reborn/Index.md>) — Six-mission campaign where the PCs help the Forlorn Hope secure its future.
- [Single Player Mode](<Single Player Mode/Index.md>) — Around 100 pages of tools, tables, and rules for solo and GM-less play.
- [Micro Chrome](<Micro Chrome/Index.md>) — Adds 4 new gear items.
- [Mixing Drinks, Changing Lives](<Mixing Drinks Changing Lives/Index.md>) — VA-11 HALL-A crossover DLC.
- [Night Market Index](<Night Market Index/Index.md>) — Living master catalog of every purchasable item, service, and gear in Cyberpunk RED with costs and source references.
- [Interface RED Vol. 1](<Interface RED Vol 1/Index.md>) — First Interface RED compilation.
- [Interface RED Vol. 2](<Interface RED Vol 2/Index.md>) — Second Interface RED compilation.
- [Interface RED Vol. 3](<Interface RED Vol 3/Index.md>) — Third Interface RED compilation.
- [Interface RED Vol. 4](<Interface RED Vol 4/Index.md>) — Fourth Interface RED compilation.
- [Interface RED Vol. 5](<Interface RED Vol 5/Index.md>) — Fifth Interface RED compilation.
