## Land Vehicles

### AmeriCar Armadillo

**Manufacturer:** AmeriCar  
**Cost:** 41,000eb (Super Luxury)

The Armadillo is the safest, strongest RV ever produced, and AmeriCar knows it! That's why the automotive neocorp didn't change a thing when it resurrected the popular caravan and offered it for sale to a new generation of travelers. It remains the vehicle of choice for both nomads living on the road and corporate campers seeking to shed stress and touch dirt.

The AmeriCar Armadillo is a Compact Groundcar with an Armored Chassis, Bulletproof Glass (×2), Heavy Chassis, Housing Upgrade, Reinforced Tires, and Seating Upgrade.

### New American Motors Ambunaught m2

**Manufacturer:** New American Motors  
**Cost:** 56,000eb (Super Luxury)

New American Motors may be dead, but its Ambunaughts still roam the streets of Night City. These decades-old medical juggernauts, held together by duct tape and creative mechanical repair tricks, form the backbone of emergency services provider REO Meatwagon's fleet. A combination of run-flat tires and hover mode ensures rigs can reach patients wherever they are, while armor and environmental seals protect paramedics and patients alike. The rear medical bay comes standard with a variety of built-in scanners and tools.

The New American Motors Ambunaught m2 is a Compact Groundcar with two seats. It comes with an Armored Chassis, Bulletproof Glass, Heavy Chassis, Hover Upgrade, Reinforced Tires, and Sealed Environment Upgrades. The medical bay holds beds for up to four patients and seats for two working paramedics. It is equipped with the following onboard equipment: Cryopumps (×2), Medscanner, Medtech Bag, Tech Bag, and Techscanner.

**Manufacturer:** TetraCorp America  
**Cost:** 45,000eb (Super Luxury)

The latest in corporate luxury, the TetraCorp America Luxus 15 drives the classic stretched limo into the modern day! Travel in confidence, knowing the armored, sealed cab will protect you from both firearms and gas attacks. Lean back in the finest faux leather seats and enjoy drinks dispensed from the built-in Drinkmaster 3000. When you travel in a Luxus 15, you travel in style!

### Zonda R400 Trail

**Manufacturer:** Zonda  
**Cost:** 850eb (Expensive)

The most basic of motorcycles, the Zonda R400 Trail is the perfect entry-level vehicle for urban commuters seeking to escape the tyranny of public transportation. Rumor has it that nomad clans keep a small fleet of R400s to use when training their teens how to drive.

The Zonda R400 Trail is a Roadbike with only one seat. It has an SDP of 16, a Combat Speed of 10 MOVE, and a Narrative Speed of 30 MPH / 48 KPH. The R400 is incompatible with the Seating Upgrade. If fitted with the AV Engine Upgrade, an R400 can fly, but its movement rates remain the same. An R400 cannot be Tech Upgraded to increase its Combat or Narrative Speed.

## New Vehicle Upgrades

*Nomad Moto Role Ability upgrades. Rank 1 upgrades are typically 1,000eb (Very Expensive) on the open market; higher-rank upgrades are 5,000eb (Luxury) unless noted.*

### Aerozep Selfsealing Gasbag

**Moto Rank Required:** 7  
**Availability:** Aerozep

Reinforces the vehicle's weak points so they cannot be targeted by an Aimed Shot.

### Hangar

**Moto Rank Required:** 7  
**Availability:** Aerozep, Yacht

Furnishes the vehicle with a safe parking space for a single non-yacht, non-aerozep vehicle. The parking space may take the form of an actual enclosed hangar, a landing pad, or a davit frame system. Passengers with access can enter/exit a parked vehicle without taking an Action. Multiple upgrades can be installed.

### Reinforced Hull/Tires

**Moto Rank Required:** 1  
**Availability:** All Land and Sea Vehicles

Reinforces the vehicle's weak points so they cannot be targeted by an Aimed Shot.

### Sealed Environment

**Moto Rank Required:** 1  
**Availability:** All Vehicles

Environmentally seals the vehicle. Anyone inside the vehicle is protected from toxic gases, fumes, and aerosolized attacks originating from outside the vehicle. If any windows on the vehicle are destroyed, this benefit is lost until they are repaired.

### Submersible Upgrade

**Moto Rank Required:** 5  
**Availability:** All Land and Sea Vehicles except Bikes and Jetskis

Waterproofs the vehicle and adds a submersible engine, allowing it to travel underwater at the speed of a Speedboat. The vehicle is piloted using the Pilot Sea Vehicle Skill while submerged.

### Titanic Hull

**Moto Rank Required:** 7  
**Availability:** Aerozep, Yacht

Increases the SDP of the vehicle by an amount that scales with the number of rooms it has.

| Rooms | SDP Bonus |
|-------|-----------|
| 1–4 | +100 SDP |
| 5–8 | +180 SDP |
| 9–12 | +250 SDP |
| 13+ | +380 SDP |

## New Vehicle Rooms

### Luxury Vehicle Room

**Cost:** Double the cost of a base room when purchased during vehicle construction

When building a vehicle that has rooms, any room may be upgraded to a Luxury Room for double the cost of that room. A Luxury Room only differs in aesthetics. A Luxury Room is a room suitable for a king, CEO, or diplomat, and everyone can tell the difference. If the room is being rented, the GM should decide what category of housing it is.

The Housing Capacity Nomad Vehicle Upgrade, when taken at Moto Rank 5 (5,000eb if purchased separately) or above, can upgrade an existing room in a vehicle to a Luxury Room instead of adding a new room. Multiple upgrades can be installed.

### Complex Vehicle Room

**Cost:** Triple the cost of a base or luxury room when purchased during vehicle construction

A Complex Room is a variant base room for a vehicle. Complex Rooms are large, can serve a variety of purposes, and add to the vehicle three times the number of seats of a typical room for the vehicle (6 for Aerozeps/Cabin Cruisers, 12 for Yachts). Restaurants, Recreation decks, Prisons, Bowling Alleys, Cargo Bays, Bunkhouses, Cafeterias, and Laser Tag Arenas are all examples of Complex Rooms.

The Housing Capacity Nomad Vehicle Upgrade, when taken at Moto Rank 7 (5,000eb if purchased separately) or above, can upgrade an existing room in a vehicle to a Complex Room instead of adding a new room. Multiple upgrades can be installed.
