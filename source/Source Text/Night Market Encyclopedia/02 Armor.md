#### Special Features

**Bodyweight Suit** — Single purchase covers Body and Head (SP 11 each). Both locations repaired together. Cannot wear more than one Bodyweight Suit or combine with similar armor/fashion (e.g. Lotos Netsuit). Adds 1 Hardware-only Cyberdeck Option Slot on the suit while worn; hardware inaccessible when suit is off.

**Executive Armor** — Nanomachines restore 1 ablated SP per day on any day the armor does not lose SP.

**Fire Brand Bunker Gear** — Single purchase; always worn on Body and Head. Both repaired together. Wearer immune to fire damage (including incendiary ammo) and cannot be set on fire. Gas mask deploys without an Action; while active, immune to inhaled toxins. Built-in O₂ tank: 30 minutes of air, 1 hour refill from atmosphere.

**Laser Light Street Jacket** — Fiber-optic displays (solid/strobe black light, red, green, white, club scene, firefight ignite when Smartgun Link connected). Counts as 1 Light Tattoo installation toward Wardrobe and Style +2 (3 Light Tattoos).

**Lotos Netsuit** — Single purchase; always worn on Body and Head (SP 13 each). Both repaired together. Cannot combine with Bodyweight Suit or similar. Cyberdeck gains 1 extra Hardware-only slot stored on the suit; hardware auto-uninstalls when suit removed. Includes Excellent Quality Cyberdeck (CP:R 351).

**Masetto AirRider** — On Whiplash Critical Injury from a crash, suit inflates to prevent it; MOVE −5 (min 1) until deflated (Action). After inflation, replace 20eb (Everyday) CO₂ cartridge before reuse.

**MechaMan Helmet** — Built-in Tech-upgraded Smart Glasses with Chyron, Low Light/Infrared/UV Cybereye options. Illuminated; HUD; interior/exterior speakers; Agent link.

**Mimic Clothing Kit** — Attach ceramic plates and ballistic segments to any Fashion Jacket or Top (one Body piece). Becomes Light Armorjack with that Fashion appearance; plates cannot be removed after installation.

**Montage Variable Clothing** — Chip preloaded with 3 colors/patterns (buyer's choice). Change display with Action via paired Agent. Additional colors/patterns: 20eb (Everyday) each, download via Agent (~1 min).

**Scavenged Armor** — Body only. Ablated SP cannot be restored. At 0 SP, armor falls off the wearer.

**Shock Armor** — Interior rubber-lined (immune to own charge). As defender in a grapple, Action to pulse electricity: attacker DV15 Resist Torture/Drugs Check or grapple ends.

**SkidRow PackShield** — Businesswear-appearance carryall when worn as backpack (no protection). Action to unfold into shield; must equip in hand for protection. Cannot install in Popup Shield cyberware.

**Street Viper Riding Suit** — Elbow blades are 2 Medium Melee Weapons, always considered drawn while suit is worn.

**Tactical Smart Armor** — Single purchase; always worn on Body and Head (SP 12 each). Both repaired together. Built-in Smart Glasses.
