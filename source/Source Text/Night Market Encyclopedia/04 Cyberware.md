#### Special Features

**Biosystem** — Permanent FBC prep: uninstalls all current cyberware (returned bagged) and meat body. Skills and INT, COOL, TECH, WILL, LUCK, EMP unchanged; BODY/HP/Death Save follow inhabited FBC. Cannot install cyberware directly—only via FBC body. Outside FBC: Cover 25 HP shell; brain dies on direct hit; observation/communication only. Monthly Glucose-Rich Brain Solution (100eb) via access port. Immune to disease, poison, pharma, street drugs (except Airhypo to port), radiation; no breathe/food/water. Implant into FBC: 4 hr, DV17 Cybertech or Surgery.

**Dragoon Plating / Metalgear** — External Body. Requires BODY 16+ unless FBC. Action to jettison plating (removes SP & penalty). Action magnetic call summons spare set within 6 m/yds. Nanomachines repair 1 ablated SP/day when no SP lost that day. Extra sets: Dragoon 1,000eb (V Expensive); Metalgear 5,000eb (Luxury). Custom colors +100eb.

**Internal Fūma Kotarō L Frame** — Requires BODY 8 + 2× Grafted Muscle and Bone Lace. Cannot raise BODY above 12. Draw/sheath without Action: 2× Excellent Exotic Heavy Melee or 1× Excellent Exotic Light + 1× Excellent Exotic Very Heavy Melee (concealed when sheathed; hand empty while drawn).

**Internal L Frame Ω (Omega)** — Requires BODY 10 + 3× Grafted Muscle and Bone Lace. Cannot raise BODY above 16. Counts as 2 separate Borgware installations for Maximum Humanity depression (therapy).

**Kiroshi MonoVision** — If MonoVision is user's only sight source, Lost Eye Critical Injury effect (including Base Death Save Penalty) is doubled.

**Zeta CyberConductor, Integrated** — FBC only. Up to 3 Cyberdecks in conductor; switch as Meat Action while Jacked In (no HP damage). Other FBC-integrated decks also link to conductor. Deck install/uninstall: 1 hr. Max 1 CyberConductor. Counts as cyberware-integrated for FBC Integrated Bodyweight Coating.
