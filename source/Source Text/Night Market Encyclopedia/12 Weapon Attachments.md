## Weapon Attachments

*Category default:* Non-Exotic ranged weapons have 3 attachment slots unless noted (CP:R 343). Attachments are weapon-type-specific; duplicate same attachment has no extra effect. Extended/Drum magazines use Clip Chart capacities (CP:R 343–344). Magazine attachments prevent concealment under clothing.

## CEMK Weapon Attachments

*Category default:* Rebuild attachments convert a non-Exotic weapon to Power, Smart, or Tech (CEMK 30–31). Install/uninstall takes 1 hr. Only one Rebuild per weapon.

| Name | Cost | Slots | Eligible | Effect | Special Mechanisms | Source |
|------|------|-------|----------|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Power Rebuild | 1,000eb (V Expensive) | 2 | Non-Exotic ranged | Converts to **Power Weapon** | Crit Injury bonus damage +5; ricochet off cover at −4 Attack (ignored on Aimed Shot) | CEMK 30 |
| Smart Rebuild | 1,000eb (V Expensive) | 2 | Non-Exotic ranged (no existing Smartgun Link) | Converts to **Smart Weapon**; +1 Ranged Attack | Loads Improved Smart Ammo; requires Interface Plugs or Subdermal Grip | CEMK 31 |
| Tech Rebuild | 1,000eb (V Expensive) | 2 | Non-Exotic ranged (not GL/RL) | Converts to **Tech Weapon** | Scope outlines targets behind Thin Cover; Move Action charges 60 sec/20 rnds; next non-explosive shot ROF 1, pierces Thin Cover, ignores half SP (cover takes no dmg) | CEMK 31 |
