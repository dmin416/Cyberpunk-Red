## Payment Rules

- Pay at the **start of each month** (CP:R 377).
- **One week grace period**, then a **Death Save** each unpaid day.
- Living **beyond your paid tier** — GM charges separately.
- **1 month = 28 days**.
- Lifestyle payment is **one bundled bill** — you don't spend extra for anything in the BC breakdown below (BC 130).

### Campaign house rules (Epics / Homebrew)

When a Tech with **HQ Workshop** fabricates:

| Rule | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Tech HQ | Fabrication DTD **÷3** |
| Materials | One tier below retail (Super Luxury = half) |
| Labor (table rate) | 50eb/DTD |

**Example — Generic Prepak food month (700eb materials, 84 hours → 1 DTD with HQ):** ~700eb + 50eb labor = **750eb** vs **300eb lifestyle**. Still not worth it for subsistence.

## Lifestyle vs Buying Outright

| Method | Kibble Tier Food | Notes |
|--------|------------------|-------|
| **Kibble Lifestyle** | **100eb** | 56 meals + Agent + entertainment + transit |
| Retail packs | 560eb | Food only |
| Tech-fab materials | 280eb | Food only; 56 hours labor |
| Tech-fab + HQ labor | ~295eb | Still ~3× lifestyle |

**Bottom line:** Pay your lifestyle. BC explicitly counts the meals — Kibble is **2/day**, every other tier is **3/day** in a fixed mix of lower-tier meals plus the tier's namesake food. Retail and fabrication only beat lifestyle if you're buying a single meal, not feeding yourself for a month.
