## 1. Ten Punk Campaign Ideas

**Source:** [Interface RED Vol 3 / Collecting the Random](../Source%20Text/Interface%20RED%20Vol%203/07%20Collecting%20the%20Random.md) · p. 53  
Supplements Core “Meet the Teams” (below). Each idea has **five** suggested missions.

### 1.1 Guerrilla Gardeners
Grow your own food — fresh produce as punk resistance to corp kibble.

1. Retrieve seeds from a lost vault.
2. Protect your stand from rivals at the Night Market.
3. Deal with Nomads for smuggled chickens.
4. Rescue your kidnapped master gardener from Biotechnica.
5. Stop a gang war before it destroys your garden.

### 1.2 Family Legacy
Siblings inherit a mcguffin locked in Mister Kernaghan’s private vault (CP:R p. 305). The heist is a long-term project.

1. Recon Mister K to find the vault.
2. Get the laser cutter needed to cut in.
3. Infiltrate his security firm and put your people on the “payroll.”
4. Negotiate with a gang to make a distraction.
5. The heist itself.

### 1.3 Librarians
Info broker Nostradamus collects books to restore knowledge after the Datakrash. Get them wherever they are.

1. Badlands trip to an abandoned college library.
2. Break into an Exec’s estate for an original *Origin of Species*.
3. Protect the boss’s collection from a Corp strike team.
4. Escort a chemist through the city to treat the collection for mold.
5. Who killed Nostradamus — and how?

### 1.4 Food Truck War
Aunt Andy’s Icelandic recipe + bio-engineered lichen = cheap delicious “meal cookies.” She needs a truck and muscle.

1. Steal a truck (Network 54 has plenty).
2. Steal a lot of Kibble (Continental Brands has tons).
3. Clear mutated rats from Andy’s lichen cave.
4. High-speed chase: gangers joyriding the stolen truck through the Old Combat Zone.
5. Debut day — rivals, best spots, serve the food.

### 1.5 Do-Gooder Clinic
Crew works at From the Embers (Phoenix Redwyne, CP:R p. 414), a genuine community clinic under constant siege.

1. Rent’s due — move all clinic gear to a new location without losing any.
2. Patient needs a heart a Body Bank won’t sell cheap — four hours to get it somehow.
3. Local booster gang wants protection money you don’t have.
4. Train the first volunteer ambulance-corp batch.
5. Phoenix Redwyne is missing. Find her.

### 1.6 Prankster Gang
Punch up: show the elites are fallible. Laughs required.

1. Recon the building you’re going to prank.
2. Supply run — a *lot* of red paint.
3. Prep break-in to lay groundwork.
4. The prank: make the building bleed.
5. Get away from the Corp cops.

### 1.7 Paracy(ber)cology
Run a Pop Media show investigating “spooky” happenings — no supernatural rules required.

1. Reclaimers want to repopulate an abandoned suburb that seems haunted.
2. Someone’s ripping people apart — cyberpsycho or werewolf?
3. Strange mushroom circles in the Badlands — fairies or Biotechnica?
4. Small-Corp CEO claims UFO abduction; hires the crew to investigate.
5. Juviegang rumors: shapeshifters, kids who hear radio waves without a radio.

### 1.8 Sapience Liberation Front
Liberate clones and AIs from Corporate control.

1. Extract an AI Network 54 uses for weather prediction.
2. A liberated clone under a new identity has vanished.
3. Mole compromised the safe house — move before Corp cops arrive.
4. Root out the mole from mission 3.
5. Mole’s intel: Soulkilled AIs stored at Ziggurat HQ — free them.

### 1.9 Mr. K's Collectors
Work for Mister Kernaghan acquiring collectibles for his Midnight Market.

1. Mob boss wears Richard Night’s class ring everywhere — get it.
2. Recover one of the world’s first cyberdecks from the old EBM building in the Hot Zone.
3. Collect 50 Bozo noses for a famous artist’s commission (they’re their *real* noses).
4. Find Mike Puddleforge for a signed first edition of *Cyborg Anarchist*.
5. Nomad pack jumped you escorting a limited-edition AV-4 — get it back.

### 1.10 The Perfect Prison
Crew wakes in suburban heaven with no memory of how they got there — and every sign they’ve always lived there.

1. A neighbor who seemed to know something has vanished.
2. One of the Crew replaced with a biosculpted double.
3. One of the Crew elected to the HOA board — use it or get corrupted.
4. People the Crew vaguely remember appear in the Suburb.
5. Escape route found — leave, or stay for the siren call?

## 2. Core — Meet the Teams

**Source:** [Core Rulebook / Screamsheets](../Source%20Text/Core%20Rulebook/17%20Screamsheets.md) · p. 390–392  
Campaign **frames** (how the Crew exists), not mission lists. IR3 Ten Punk Ideas supplement these.

| Team | One-line pitch |
|------|----------------|
| **Corporate Teams** | Work a Corp’s goals from its offices/security; Exec + Netrunner + Fixer + Tech + Solos. |
| **Bands** | Rockerboys on the road — gigs, trouble, hell-raising; Fixer manager, Tech, Solo roadies. |
| **Cults / Boostergangs** | Players *are* the gang — Street outlet to kill, loot, belong. |
| **Reclaimers** | Rebuild a homestead in abandoned Old America; Nomad lead, Solos/Fixers protect supplies. |
| **Trauma Teams** | Licensed paramedics in a minigun AV-4 ambulance; Medtechs + security + optional Media. |
| **Mercenaries** | Hired dirty-work crew; Fixer contracts, Netrunner, Tech, Medtech, Solos/Nomads. |
| **Gangs** | Neighborhood defense / resistance / Robin Hood — not just enemy fodder. |
| **Nomad Packs** | Caravan family; Fixers for in-town deals, Netrunner intel, Techs/Medtechs, Nomads/Solos. |
| **Lawmen** | Precinct, Psycho Squad, road marshals, or private investigation firm. |
| **Media Teams** | Anywhere for the story — reporters, Netrunner, Tech, muscle, harried Exec upstairs. |

## 3. Core — Side Quest Ideas

**Source:** same · p. 394

1. Experimental tech falls into the Crew’s hands; the Corp/Tech who built it hires Solos to take it back.
2. NCPD hires the Crew to hunt a stealthy, methodical cyberpsycho killing civilians.
3. Nomad’s Pack says a close family member went rogue — hunt them, or find out what happened.
4. New musical act hires the Crew as muscle/support for Night City gigs; build the band and manager as recurring NPCs.

## 4. Danger Gal Dossier+ — Faction Plot Hooks

**Source:** [Danger Gal Dossier+ / Faction Plot Hooks](../Source%20Text/Danger%20Gal%20Dossier+/01%20Faction%20Plot%20Hooks.md) · p. 2–5  
Three hooks per faction (some have four). Spin into missions, side quests, or background noise.

### 6th Street
- **Contracts, Contracts, Contracts:** Neocorp start-up offer looks too good / untraceable — off-books crew digs up the founder.
- **Cyber-Serial Killer:** Surgical, inhuman-speed kills every four days; 6th Street wants the killer before MAX-TAC steals glory.
- **NCPD Strikes:** Undercover cops escalate to frame 6th Street for an NCART bomb; Del (Zoner) overheard the plan.

### Bozos
- **Big Top, Big Money:** Militech pays to take Big Top down publicly with Militech gear after ugly Data Pool footage.
- **Bozo Bounty:** NCPD reward for high-ranking Bozos — alive only.
- **Oh God! Clowns!:** Smooth night brick-run until the Bozos show up.

### Danger Gal Puma Squad
- **Fear and Loathing in Otaku City:** Bodyguard an ELO developer at Nightkon in cosplay while Puma hunts a PK ringleader.
- **Fearful Symmetry:** Jane Doe on the beach is Lynx’s genetic twin; Lynx is fine — investigate compromise.
- **Please, Puma – Leave those Kids Alone:** Cat-eared lady helping YoGangers — altruism or recruitment?

### Digital Divas
- **DMSketchy:** Why is DMS grooming Sizzle Jams?
- **Stealing the Music:** Who’s stealing from band provider Matt?
- **Unanswered Questions:** Why did Bev Hatric start fires after Divas shows?

### Edgerunners
- **Data Gather:** Danger Gal hires discreet intel (and staged field tests) on dossier Edgerunners.
- **Data Merge:** Acquire Crasher / CrAB Lord files — buy, steal, or get played by sellers and rivals.
- **Data Theft:** Stolen dossiers used for blackmail; race for the files (Tetsuo’s new identity at stake).

### Generation Red
- **Do You Know Where Your Kid Is?:** Adult guardian wants their kid back from the Old Combat Zone.
- **Help a Kid Out:** City or bigger boostergang pressure — kids need creative (or lucky) outside help.
- **Help a Kid Up?:** Danger Gal recruiting; pressure may push Apex toward DG or unite kids against outsiders.

### Maelstrom
- **Burn Notice:** Detective Mauser pays to zero a dirty cop before they out his Maelstrom informant.
- **Dead Guys Is Parts:** Maelstrom buys scarred/Nuke-burned cyber — working preferred.
- **Lebentanz:** Flenser wants a new Totentanz home in Watson; bonus for “motivated sellers.”

### Network 54
- **Escape from Night City:** Fiona wants to fake her death and leave; Net54 may pay more to bring her in.
- **Hostage Situation!:** Fiona’s team held by Maelstrom; Net54 won’t pay ransom — hire retrieval instead.
- **Pay the Price:** Convince a Petrochem Exec to pay blackmail to kill a fertilizer-theft story.
- **Will the Real Fiona Please Stand Up?:** Fiona lookalike sightings; Angle wants her dead if she’s alive.

### NCPD Precinct #1
- **Block Party:** Cop order to clear Reclaimers for Zhirafa vs. municipal grant claim — help whom?
- **The Competition:** Your Upper Marina Exec op collides with an NCPD detective unit on the same target.
- **New Drug, Who This?:** Gang clashes spike in the Old CZ — rumors of a new Street drug.

### Piranhas
- **Blood in the Waters:** Piranhas at every Edgerunner party hunting whoever stole Dirty Shirley’s formula.
- **A Different Kind of Party:** Extra fee for bloodier parties — victims may be kidnapped elite.
- **The Party Girl:** Get Mister Kernighan’s heir Epiphany out of a three-month party haze (or help them run).

### Sightseers
- **Digging for Dirt:** Trace Santiago hires investigation of Sarge’s squad after Endo/Swirl treatment.
- **Flatline and Frame Up:** Sarge hires flatline of Pitbull framed on a local gang.
- **Jailbreak:** Help Racer’s crew break Endo and Swirl out without implicating the Aldecaldos.
- **Pick a Side:** Nomad guerrilla strike on a precinct — assist or intervene.

### Team Monster
- **Bunny Business:** Subatomics want evidence of unethical Biotechnica island research; bar rumors of death rabbits.
- **Missing Musicians:** Paradox singers vanish after elite private-party gigs; Nox Arya may know something.
- **Rival Crews:** Recover cargo from sea Raffen Shiv — Team Monster is on the same job.

### Trauma Team Squad NC4-2
- **Help Me Find My Lost Cousin?:** “Grieving relative” job pointing at Stick is actually a hit on Twist.
- **Landlord Trouble:** Protection racketeers lean on Penitent and Paddles; someone you care about is in the crossfire.
- **Trauma Card Fraud:** Fake TT bills from NC4-2; clear debt collectors and get the squad paid.

### Tyger Claws
- **Deliver with Tyger Eats!:** Quick-cash meal (and maybe extra) delivery for rent money.
- **The Invisible Hand:** Danger Gal wants Arasaka sleeper Kusa in the Claws/Japanese community taken out.
- **Repel the Land Sharks:** Continental Brands Oasis + hired muscle vs. local food stalls — pick a side.

### Zoners
- **The Cybermole:** Movement plans leaking; FreeFall calls in a favor to find the spy.
- **NCPD Crackdown:** Council incentives for NCPD harassment — who’s using the cops as a tool?
- **We are Night City!:** Overturned Petrochem transport in South NC — cargo for defense, food, or leverage.
